## SECTION 3: Solution / How It Works

### Section Header

> Describe it. Generate it. Refine it.

### Body

AgentStudio works through conversation. You describe what you need in plain language, and an AI assistant handles the technical decisions -- selecting the best model for your request, writing an optimized prompt, and setting the right parameters automatically.

### Steps

**1. Describe your vision**
Type what you need in the chat. "Design a minimalist logo for a coffee shop called Brew & Bean." That is all it takes.

**2. Get your image**
The AI selects the right model, crafts the prompt, and generates your image in a dedicated workspace alongside the conversation.

**3. Refine with words**
Want changes? Say so. "Make the font bolder and change the color to deep brown." The system switches to image editing mode automatically, preserving your original composition while applying your changes.

**4. Browse every version**
Every iteration is saved. Navigate back through previous versions, compare changes side by side, and download any version you want.
