## SECTION 3: Solution / How It Works

### Section Header

> Describe it. Generate it. Refine it.

### Body

AgentStudio works through conversation. You describe what you need in plain language, and an AI assistant handles the technical decisions -- selecting the best model for your request, writing an optimized prompt, and setting the right parameters automatically.

### Steps

**1. Describe your vision**
Type what you need in the chat. "Design a minimalist logo for a coffee shop called Brew & Bean." That is all it takes.

**2. Get your image**
The AI selects the right model, crafts the prompt, and generates your image in a dedicated workspace alongside the conversation.

**3. Refine with words**
Want changes? Say so. "Make the font bolder and change the color to deep brown." The system switches to image editing mode automatically, preserving your original composition while applying your changes.

**4. Browse every version**
Every iteration is saved. Navigate back through previous versions, compare changes side by side, and download any version you want.

## Annotations

### Hero Headline: "Turn words into professional images."
Uses the "Turn {input} into {outcome}" formula. Communicates the core value proposition -- text-to-image transformation -- in five words. "Professional" differentiates from casual AI art tools and speaks directly to the target audience of designers, marketers, and creative professionals.

### Problem Section
Follows the principle of articulating the problem better than the customer can. Addresses three real alternatives (designers, stock photography, complex AI tools) and the friction with each. Avoids fabricated statistics -- uses observable truths instead.

### How It Works: 4 Steps
Slightly exceeds the recommended 3-step format because the version history capability is a genuine differentiator that warrants its own step. Each step uses a simple verb and describes an outcome.

### Model Section: Full Detail
The user specifically requested full details about every AI model. This section is intentionally comprehensive. Each model entry follows the same pattern: name, capability summary, and best-use-case guidance. Premium models are clearly marked.

### Final CTA
"Your next image is a conversation away." -- Reinforces the chat-based workflow (the product's key differentiator) and creates forward momentum. The single-sentence body below it recaps the three core value propositions: model selection, parameter handling, and prompt crafting.

### CTA Button: "Sign In"
Per the brief, the primary CTA is "Sign In" linking to the sign-in page. This is used consistently in both the hero and closing sections. While the copywriting guidelines suggest more descriptive CTAs (e.g., "Create Your First Image"), the brief explicitly specifies "Sign In" as the action.
